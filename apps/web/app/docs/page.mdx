## Chapter 9: FAQ

### What makes OMENA different?

OMENA focuses on intelligence infrastructure rather than historical indexing.

### Does OMENA execute transactions?

No. OMENA is an intelligence layer only.

### How does OMENA maintain low latency?

Custom mempool tracking nodes and validator-adjacent processing clusters.
